Welcome to CrossFit Nova Vita: "New Life"

We train using Crossfit methodologies. 

By use of constantly varied functional movements, executed at high intensity, we train to increase our work capacities across broad time and modal domains.Our exercise routines are even scalable to meet the needs of all athletes; beginner, advanced, and expert alike. Like many CrossFit gyms, we teach basic to advanced classes in functional exercise, athletic conditioning, plyometrics, kettlebell training, Olympic weightlifting, medicine ball and dumbbell training, power-lifting, gymnastics, rowing, running and jumping rope. 

 

We pride ourselves on how we teach. 

We believe in the integrity of movement, the safety of our students, and a steady progress that develops not just athleticism, but a high quality of life. It is the physical embodiment of ones character, traits of honor, courage, confidence, discipline, integrity, responsibility, dedication, and joy.
